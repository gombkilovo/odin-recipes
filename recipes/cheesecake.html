How its made: Cheesecake

Back to main page

Cheesecake is a rich and creamy dessert made from soft cheese, eggs, sugar, and a crumb crust. Its origins date back to ancient Greece, where it was served to athletes during the first Olympic Games in 776 BC. The American version of cheesecake was developed in the 19th century with the addition of cream cheese, and today, it is a popular dessert around the world served in many different variations.
